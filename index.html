GITAM Deemed to be University was established by Mr. M.V.V.S. Murthy. Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management, formerly GITAM University and GITAM College of Engineering, is an institute of higher education with campuses in Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad and Bengaluru in India. It was established in 1980 at Visakhapatnam, it was affiliated to Andhra University.
